Soccer: more than just a game

Summary

  1. A game is 90 minutes
  2. Every time the ball goes in the opposition's net you get a point
  3. Team with the most point wins
  4. 11 players per team
  5. If a foul is committed the referee can give a yellow card or a red card
  6. Red card = expulsion. Two yellow cards = red Card

Why is it so loved?

Everyone can play not matter their size
